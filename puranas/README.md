# पुराण

| **#** | **Title** | **What it is about** |
| :---: | :---: | :---: |
| 1 | ब्रह्म पुराण | Creation, cosmology, and sacred geography including Puri and Konark |
| 2 | पद्म पुराण | Devotion to Viṣṇu, creation stories, and pilgrimage sites including Prayāga and Vārāṇasī |
| 3 | विष्णु पुराण | Viṣṇu's ten avatāras, cosmic order, and the Daśāvatāra including Rāma and Kṛṣṇa |
| 4 | शिव पुराण | Glory of Śiva, symbolism of the liṅga, and Śaiva cosmology |
| 5 | भागवत पुराण | Life of Kṛṣṇa, bhakti teachings, and the soul's journey toward liberation |
| 6 | नारद पुराण | Rituals, devotion, sacred calendars, and the duties of a devotee |
| 7 | मार्कण्डेय पुराण | Home of the Devī Māhātmya (Durgā Saptaśatī) — the glory of the goddess and dharma across cosmic cycles |
| 8 | अग्नि पुराण | An encyclopedia — temple architecture, iconography, statecraft, yoga, medicine, and ritual |
| 9 | भविष्य पुराण | Prophecies about future ages, coming kings, and the unfolding of Kali Yuga |
| 10 | ब्रह्मवैवर्त पुराण | Divine love of Rādhā and Kṛṣṇa, cosmology, and the nature of Prakṛti |
| 11 | लिङ्ग पुराण | Origins of the Śiva liṅga, Śaiva cosmogony, and the destruction of Tripurāsura |
| 12 | वाराह पुराण | Viṣṇu's Varāha (boar) avatāra, sacred tīrthas, and Vaiṣṇava devotional practice |
| 13 | स्कन्द पुराण | The largest Purāṇa — Kārttikeya's birth and exploits, sacred geography, and pilgrimage |
| 14 | वामन पुराण | Viṣṇu's Vāmana (dwarf) avatāra, dharma, and the defeat of King Bali |
| 15 | कूर्म पुराण | Viṣṇu's Kūrma (tortoise) avatāra, the churning of the cosmic ocean, and Śaiva philosophy |
| 16 | मत्स्य पुराण | Viṣṇu's Matsya (fish) avatāra, the great flood, lunar dynasties, and sacred sites Kāśī and Prayāga |
| 17 | गरुड पुराण | The soul's journey after death, karma, the afterlife, ancestral rites, and gemology |
| 18 | ब्रह्माण्ड पुराण | Cosmic egg (brahmāṇḍa), genealogies, the Lalitā Sahasranāma, and Śākta teachings |
